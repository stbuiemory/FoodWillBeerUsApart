Our Brewpub Garlic Fries Seasoning, with the pungent aroma and flavor of garlic at the front of this blend, is a compendium of kitchen aromatics and a shortcut to great-tasting food. Blended with garlic, onion, salt, and white pepper, this blend is sleek and savory. The garlic comes forward first, and then this blend presents a balanced showcase of salt and onion, kept in line by the clean, straightforward burn of white pepper. This seasoning blend is mixed in small batches in our facility, to protect the flavor and freshness in our products.
